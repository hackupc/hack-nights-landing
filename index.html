What

HackNights are born with the aim of offering a collaborative work space where you can work freely in what you like the most. A place where you learn, grow and teach in community. No prize, no competition. Only you, your friends and your projects.

Where

HackNights are held at Aula d’Estudis in Campus Nord, next to the Rector Gabriel Ferraté library.
